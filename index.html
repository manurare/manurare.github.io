Manuel Rey-Area


I am a PhD candidate at University of Bath under the supervision of Christian Richardt. I'm interested in virtual reality, computational imaging and low-level computer vision.

I coursed my MSc at Autonomous University of Barcelona supervised by Javier Ruiz-Hidalgo. I did my thesis on whale detection working with WWF under an ESA summer programme.

I completed my Bachelors in Telecommunications Engineering at University of Vigo with Julio Martín-Herrero.
